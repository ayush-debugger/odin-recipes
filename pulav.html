pulav

Description

Pulao (or pilaf) is a popular one-pot South Asian rice dish where basmati rice is cooked with broth, aromatic spices, and vegetables, meat, or dry fruits. It is known for its light, aromatic, and less spicy flavor compared to biryani, usually prepared by simmering ingredients until the water is absorbed

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Masala

Steps

  1. Boil Rice
  2. Prepare the vegetables
  3. Prepare the Masala Mixture
  4. Mix Everything
  5. Heat on and Rest
